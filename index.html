Who doesn’t want a robot to do all their boring work?

Web copywriters and email marketers go back and forth between Word and Sublime Text all day, every day—and they usually waste a lot of time typing out special characters, otherwise marking-up text, and manually refreshing Chrome.

Together with some like-minded friends, I came up with a solution for this problem: SublimeScript. This friendly robot takes care of the boring, mindless work that no one likes doing.
